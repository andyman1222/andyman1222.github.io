In the Hindu and Buddhist traditions, it is believed that everyone is reborn into a new life when they die. Hinduism stemmed from Upanishadic beliefs, which concluded that everyone is essentially god, and the world is an illusion. As a result of this illusion, everyone is reborn acter they die. Known as samsara, many Hindus strived to break this cycle of rebirth by trying to understand more about their world and their minds, through yoga and meditation. After experiencing suffering, death, and meditation, the Enlightened One, or Buddha, discovered the path to Enlightenment.
