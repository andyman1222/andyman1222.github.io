Get Over It In VR

Screenshots

"Get Over It In VR" is an experimental VR game I was testing to make in Unreal Engine 4. Based off the popular game "Getting Over It," "Get Over It In VR" puts the player in a pot and requires them to navigate by pushing a hammer against the ground.

The game is only a concept, as I'm not entirely sure how to accomplish the physics of the hammer to realisticly move the player around (for example, to cling on a cliffside). However, I may change the game in the future to experiment with other gameplay mechanics only capable in VR.

However, due to moving away to college, I have been unable to work effectively on the game, or anything similar in VR.
