Space Bar/One Touch Defender

Space Bar Defender is my solo submission for the 2021 Epic Games Megajam, with the theme "Running out of Space". The theme may typically imply "space" as in physical room in an environment, or storage within a computer, container, etc., or outer space. I chose to explore the idea of "running out of space" as in running out of the spacebar on a keyboard. My idea was to limit the user to a single key for the entire game- the spacebar- where they would have a limited amount of time which the key can be pressed down.

After the game jam I have chosen to continue working on the game, including implementing an infinite waves mode, and porting it to Android. The game is now currently available on Google Play, playable similar to the PC version but using the screen instead of the spacebar.

To take this idea into a game, I chose to create an outer space-themed linear tower defense game. The player is given a number of turrets and a lane of slots to place said turrets, to defend their homeworld from incoming aliens. When holding the spacebar, the next available turret moves down the lane of slots, highlighting slots which the turretb can be placed in. Upon releasing the spacebar, the turret will settle down in the highlighted slot, unless it is already occupied by another turret, or a "squatter" enemy. When all turrets are placed, a wave of aliens travel in front of the lane, getting damaged by each turret. Upon reaching the end of the lane, the aliens will invade the planet, reducing the level of "The Space Bar," which acts as the health of the planet, as well as a meter for usage of the spacebar key. During a wave, the player can also activate the "Super Space Laser" by mashing the spacebar, which will fire a powerful laser beam, at the cost of The Space Bar's health.
