Speaker 3D model





I designed this base for speakers a sibling has. The speakers came with their own base, but they were some cheap water fountain speakers that had sound issues like static, and in general didn't sound great. I decided to 3D model and print this new base, using dimensions from the new speaker drivers and the original circuitry and fountain tech. There were issues of incorrect sizing and underestimating the amount of platic used to print while trying to make the new bases, but once it all got working the sound did improve and the speakers looked cooler than before.
