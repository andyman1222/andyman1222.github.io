CP_city_beta


Note: You can play the map yourself by subscribing to the workshop item and opening it in Team Fortress 2.

CP_city_beta is a map I made for the game Team Fortress 2 a few years ago. Originally made as a part of a summer camp, the map is designed around multiple large buildings in a city. The map uses Team Fortress 2's special "Mann Power" gamemode to take advantage of the tall buildings with a grapple hook. The map isn't entirely themed around Team Fortress 2, so in the future I plan on replacing many textures and improving the designs of the buildings, as well as adding more detail throughout the map.
