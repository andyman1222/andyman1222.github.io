Christmas scene



Bonus: hand controlled christmas lights

A neat little holiday themed scene I worked on a bit over the past few holiday breaks, based on my family's living room area. The objective of this, apart from celebrating the holidays, was to see how much VFX I could create procedurally, rather than with textures- all the vfx particles are made with a procedural material rather than a UV mapped texture, and the lighting is produced by the particles.

Features:

  • made in Unreal Engine 5 with Lumen, Nanite, Niagara
  • Procedurally generated textures create the tree topper, branches, and garland
  • A blend of gently twinkling red and green lights produce a soft golden glow
  • Fire made procedurally, with licking flames and gentle blue and orange glows
  • Snow that blows in the wind and sticks to the ground, blocking out the cloudy moonscape
  • Map layout, lighting environment, and all particle VFX created by myself
  • Some models and textures are 3rd party assets
