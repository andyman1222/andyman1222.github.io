'First Contact'

Submission

Submission for the first Unreal Engine lighting challenge, "Better Light than Never". The scene is a spaceship landing onto an alien planet, with humans making first contact with the native alien species. I manually tuned the lighting to look ideal with a two sun sunset, and also set up the scene with the intent of showing that these aliens are industrialized and pollute their planet like humans. The custom alien and spaceship models aren't the best, but the logo for the ship was custom made. Finally, I created multiple Niagara particles to create the fairy-like flies and also the glowing spots in the ground.
